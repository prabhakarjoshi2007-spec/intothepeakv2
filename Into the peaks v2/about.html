The Journey from Srinagar Garhwal

Into The Peaks was founded with a single mission: to provide an authentic window into the unexplored beauty of the Garhwal Himalayas. Based right here in Srinagar Garhwal, near the HNBGU Birla Campus, we started as a small group of local mountaineers who wanted to share their home with the world.

We believe that a trek is more than just a walk in the woods; it's a spiritual and physical reconnection with nature. Our roots at Friends Book Depot have always kept us connected to the youth and the spirit of adventure that defines this region.

What We Stand For

Local Empowerment

We exclusively hire local guides and support staff from Garhwal, ensuring that tourism benefits the community directly.

Authentic Food

From Mandua ki Roti to Jhangora ki Kheer, we serve authentic local cuisine on our treks to give you a true taste of Uttarakhand.

Certified Safety

Our leaders are trained at NIM (Nehru Institute of Mountaineering) and carry advanced first-aid kits on every journey.
